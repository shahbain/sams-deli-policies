Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

Simply browse our menu, add items to your cart, choose pickup or delivery, and proceed to checkout. You'll need to create an account using your phone number for secure authentication.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards through our secure payment processor, Stripe. You can save your payment methods for faster checkout on future orders.

How does delivery work?

Select delivery at checkout and enter your delivery address. You'll see the estimated delivery time and any delivery fees before confirming your order. Track your order status in real-time through the app.

Can I schedule a pickup order?

Yes! Choose pickup at checkout and you'll receive a notification when your order is ready. Pick up your order at our location during business hours.

How do loyalty points work?

Earn points with every purchase! Points can be redeemed for discounts on future orders. Check your points balance in the app's profile section.

Can I modify or cancel my order?

Contact us immediately at +1 (315) 520-1836 if you need to modify or cancel your order. We'll do our best to accommodate your request if the order hasn't been prepared yet.

I forgot my password. How do I sign in?

Our app uses phone number authentication - no password needed! Simply enter your phone number and verify the code sent to you via SMS.

How do I update my delivery address?

Go to your profile in the app and update your address information. You can also enter a different address at checkout for individual orders.

Is my payment information secure?

Absolutely! All payments are processed through Stripe, a PCI-DSS compliant payment processor. We never store your full card details on our servers. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

What if there's a problem with my order?

We strive for 100% customer satisfaction. If there's any issue with your order, please contact us immediately at +1 (315) 520-1836 or email samsdeliandmarket@gmail.com and we'll make it right.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account through the app settings or by contacting us at samsdeliandmarket@gmail.com. All your personal data will be permanently removed from our systems.

Technical Support

Experiencing technical issues with the app? Here are some quick troubleshooting steps:

  • Make sure you have the latest version of the app installed
  • Try closing and reopening the app
  • Check your internet connection
  • Restart your device
  • Clear the app cache (in your device settings)

If the problem persists, please contact us with details about the issue and your device type.
